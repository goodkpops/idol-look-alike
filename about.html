About This Project

An AI-powered K-Pop idol look-alike finder built for fans worldwide.

What Is This?

K-Pop Idol Look-Alike is a free, browser-based tool that uses artificial intelligence to analyze your facial features and match them to a K-Pop idol. It's built for fans who've ever wondered — "Which idol do I look like?"

The tool is entirely for entertainment purposes. Results are generated by facial feature analysis and do not represent scientific or genetic similarity.

How It Works

  1. You upload a photo or take a selfie directly in your browser.
  2. Face detection AI locates your face in the image.
  3. Feature analysis estimates your age, gender, and dominant facial expressions.
  4. Matching algorithm compares these features against our database of 24 K-Pop idols across 11 groups, and returns your closest matches with a similarity percentage.

Your Privacy Comes First

All AI processing happens directly in your browser using the open-source face-api.js library. Your photo is never uploaded to any server, never stored, and never shared.

Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Technology

face-api.js Open-source browser-based face detection and analysis
TensorFlow.js Underlying ML engine powering on-device inference
Wikipedia REST API Publicly licensed idol reference photos
Vanilla JS + CSS No frameworks — fast, lightweight, accessible

Idol Database

We currently include 24 idols from 11 major K-Pop groups:

BTS · BLACKPINK · aespa · NewJeans · IVE · TWICE · Stray Kids · NCT · EXO · TXT · (G)I-DLE

We plan to expand the database regularly. Idol profile photos are sourced from Wikipedia and are used under CC BY-SA license.
